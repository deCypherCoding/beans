Directions

From Grand Central Terminal in Manhattan

  1. Grab the 6 uptown to the stop.
  2. Transfer to the E train.
  3. Get off at the end of the line.
  4. Head East on Archer Ave and follow the signs.

From Brooklyn

  1. Get on the J and the Z train to Queens.
  2. Get off at Jamaican Center.
  3. Get off at the end of the line.
